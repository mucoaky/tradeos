## 📖 Overview

TradeOS is an AI-powered investment platform designed to simplify algorithmic trading for everyone.

Unlike traditional trading bots, TradeOS is built around transparency, risk management, and explainable decision making.

The user does not need to understand technical indicators or market analysis.

Simply:

- Connect your Binance account
- Define your investment budget
- Select your risk profile
- Let TradeOS manage the rest

Every investment decision is backed by technical analysis and accompanied by a clear explanation.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Our mission is to make algorithmic investing accessible to everyone through artificial intelligence, automation, and explainable financial decisions.

---

## 🚀 Vision

To become the operating system for intelligent investing.

TradeOS aims to combine portfolio management, AI analysis, automated trading, strategy building, backtesting, and risk management into one unified platform.
